Our circus is proud to offer a vibrant Circus School that extends a warm welcome to school groups, adults, and children alike. Whether you're an educational institution looking for a unique field trip experience or an individual seeking a fun and active way to learn circus arts, our school is open to you. Operating from Monday to Saturday, we provide a diverse range of classes and workshops that cater to various skill levels and interests, ensuring an exciting and inclusive learning environment for all ages. Join us and embark on a thrilling journey into the magical world of circus arts!

Please feel free to reach out to us at cirkat@gmail.com to express your interest and specify the type of activity you'd like to register for. We're here to assist you in finding the perfect fit for your circus arts journey. Please don't hesitate to drop us an email, and we'll be delighted to guide you through the registration process.
